Hewlett-Packard is also known as hp. HP (Hewlett-Packard) is a multinational information technology (IT) company that sells hardware, software and related business services. The company introduced handheld calculators, the first commercially distributed data processing system, LaserJet printers, and (jointly with Intel) the Explicitly Parallel Instruction Computing (EPIC) architecture.
